Maggi Noodles

Maggi is a quick and delicious noodle dish that's perfect for a fast meal. Hre's a simple recipe:

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Maggi noodles
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots,)
  • Maggi masala (included in the pack)

Method

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot and add Maggi noodles. Cook until noodlesare soft, stir it with spoon.
  2. Now, Sprinkle the Maggi masala over the noodles and mix well.
  3. Also don't forget to add teh vegetables
  4. Your delicious Maggi noodles are ready to be served!

Notes

Maggi Masala:
Adjust the quantity of Maggi masala according to your taste preferences.
customize:
Feel free to customize your Maggi by adding ingredients like boiled eggs,boiled chicken, or cheese.
